About

Built to operate.

Mostest was founded on two decades of operating experience. Its roots are in Contegix, a managed hosting and cloud infrastructure company co-founded in 2004, grown to more than 350 employees and over $100M in revenue running regulated infrastructure for customers who could not afford for it to fail, and exited in late 2024.

Today the work takes two shapes under one roof. Mostest Advisory provides fractional CTO and technology advisory services to executive teams, boards, and investors. Mostest Ventures builds and operates products of its own, and invests in startups where operating experience gives the company an edge. The advisory work keeps the building honest, and the building keeps the advice honest.

The name

Mostest is a Porter family word. It stands for a simple idea: get the most out of everything you are given, your time, your ability, and your chances. The name is personal. The standard it sets is professional, and it applies to every engagement and every product that carries it.
